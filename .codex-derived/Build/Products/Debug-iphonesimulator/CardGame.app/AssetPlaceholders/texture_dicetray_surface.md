A tileable tray-floor texture for the 3D dice tray surface.

Preferred size:

- 512x512 minimum
- 1024x1024 preferred if memory cost stays reasonable

Art direction:

- dark basalt / felt / dusted stone hybrid surface
- faint engraved rings, survey marks, or relic inlay that echo the expedition tone
- slight brass or gilt wear near the edges, but not in the quiet center play area
- worn, tactile, and premium rather than noisy or glossy

Requirements:

- tile cleanly with no visible seam
- keep the center visually calmer than the edges so dice faces remain readable
- support warm lighting and subtle specular response
- avoid bold symbols or high-contrast lines that compete with the dice
